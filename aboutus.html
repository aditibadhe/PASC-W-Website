MISSION STATEMENT

Association for Computing Machinery's Council on Women in Computing is an exclusive woman's only wing of ACM which supports & commemorates the participation, contribution and achievements of women in the technical domain.

ACM-W supports and advocates internationally for the full engagement of women in all aspects of the computing field, providing a wide range of programs and services to ACM women members and working in the community to advance the contributions of technical women, Encouraging gender equality and women empowerment.


PICT ACM-WOMEN STUDENT CHAPTER

The PICT ACM STUDENT CHAPTER FOR WOMEN is established as the sister body of PASC in a year 2019 with an aim to emancipate women and boost them in technological and computing field. PASC-W furnishes the technical skills and provides the platform where women can compete with the world. The utmost priority of this chapter is advancement of women and nurturing women with equal rights and opportunities.
